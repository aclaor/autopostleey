FAQ

Questions answered

Do I need to write the posts myself? ▾
No! Autopostleey's AI writes every post based on your business profile. You just tell it what your business does, your tone, and what to promote — it handles the rest. You can also review posts before they go live if you prefer.
Will my accounts get blocked for auto-posting? ▾
No. We follow each platform's safe posting limits — maximum 2 posts/day on Facebook and Instagram, 1/day on LinkedIn. Posts are spaced naturally so they never look like spam. Thousands of businesses post on schedule every day without issues.
Which platforms are supported? ▾
Facebook Pages, Instagram Business, LinkedIn Pages, Twitter/X, Discord (server channels) and Telegram channels. More platforms coming soon.
Can I still write posts manually? ▾
Absolutely. You can use Full Auto (AI does everything), AI Assist (AI drafts, you approve), or Manual (you write, we schedule and post). Mix and match however you like.
How does the free plan work? ▾
The free plan gives you 10 posts per month on Facebook — forever, no credit card needed. It's a great way to try Autopostleey before committing to a paid plan.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes, cancel anytime with no questions asked. Monthly subscriptions, no long-term commitment.
