FAQ
Common questions
How does AI AutoPoster work?

You fill in your business name, description, and website once. Then enable AI AutoPoster and choose your frequency (every 4 hours, daily, weekly, etc). Cloudflare's AI generates posts based on your business, takes screenshots of your website, and publishes to all your connected platforms automatically — even while you sleep.

Which platforms can I post to?

Autopostleey supports 13 platforms: Facebook Page, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Telegram, Discord, Bluesky, Threads, Pinterest, Tumblr, Google Business, and Facebook Groups. Connect each platform once and all your auto-posts go there automatically.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No! The free plan is completely free with no credit card required. You get 30 posts per month across all 13 platforms. Upgrade to Pro anytime when you need more.

Can I post to my personal Instagram or Facebook?

For Instagram Business and Facebook Pages — yes, fully automated. For personal profiles, we use a "copy & open" approach: AI writes your caption, copies it to your clipboard, and opens the app. You just paste and post in 10 seconds.

What kind of content does the AI write?

The AI rotates through different content types: product highlights, educational tips, engagement questions, promotional posts, and website showcase posts. Every post is unique and tailored to your business. You can also upload images and the AI analyzes them to write relevant captions.

Can I create Reels and Shorts?

Yes! The Reels & Shorts Creator lets you upload product photos and turn them into animated video reels with background music and text overlays. Or upload a video and AI generates a caption for it. Export and post to Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Facebook Reels, and more.
