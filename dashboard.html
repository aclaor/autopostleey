❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How does AI AutoPoster work?
Enable AI AutoPoster, set your frequency, and Autopostleey will automatically generate and post content to all your connected platforms — 24/7, even when you're asleep!
Which platforms are supported?
Autopostleey supports 13 platforms: Facebook, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Telegram, Discord, Bluesky, Threads, Pinterest, Tumblr, Google Business, and Facebook Groups.
Is Autopostleey free?
Yes! Autopostleey has a free plan that lets you post to all platforms. Premium plans unlock more features like higher posting frequency, priority support, and advanced analytics.
How do I connect my social media accounts?
Go to the Connections page in the dashboard. Click Connect next to each platform and follow the authorization steps. Most platforms connect in under 60 seconds.
Can I schedule posts in advance?
Absolutely! Use Bulk Schedule to queue up weeks or months of content at once. Set your dates, frequency, and let Autopostleey handle the rest.
